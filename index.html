CSS is very important in documents or web pages because it is used for styling the web page, to make it look more attractive. For better understanding, we can say, html is just the bone of a web page while css is the flesh. That is how important css is!

In CSS, we have what we call the box model, which includes margin, padding, and border. Padding - The inner space between the content and the border of your box. Border - The perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line like the green one pictured above. Margin - The outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.
